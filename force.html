force (completed in collaboartion with gil jang) is an abstract sculpture that explores the transition
and juxtaposition between two tools force parallels the distinction of two actions (a clean chop and
a rough press) through the transition of thin extended blades that sever the material structure to
nested masses that dominate and ground the base

the ending “twist” of motion reflects the rotational motion of the intial tool and suggests a sense of
flight and weightlessness to draw attention to the contrast with the massive bottom

the created space is repetitive, evolving, and reflects both the idea of void and of power,
symbolizing both the ice crusher and the vegatable chopper
